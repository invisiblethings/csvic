:: Christian Science Committee on Publication

Committees on Publication are members of The Mother Church (The First Church of Christ Scientist) throughout the world who are appointed to provide accurate information about Christian Science to the public – including the media, educators, and legislators. Anyone may contact the Committee by phone, fax, or e-mail.
